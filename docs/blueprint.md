# **App Name**: Primezone Computer Education

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Display the home page with a tagline, highlights, and calls to action.
- About Us Page: Display information about the institution, including its mission, features, and services.
- Diploma Courses Page: Display a catalog of diploma courses with descriptions and career paths.
- Short-Term Courses Page: Display a catalog of short-term courses with descriptions and target audience.
- Contact Us Page: Provide contact information, including phone number, email, and services offered.
- Gallery Page: Showcase images related to the institution and its activities.
- Download Brochure Feature: Allow users to download the course brochure for detailed information.
- Why Choose Us Section: Highlight the institution's commitment, focus, and goal.
- Course Catalog: Display comprehensive course information, including syllabus, duration, and instructor details.
- Course Enrollment: Allow users to sign up for courses and manage their enrollment.
- Contact and Support: Provide a contact form for inquiries and support.
- Success Stories: Showcase student testimonials and success stories.
- Image Gallery: Enable users to browse a gallery of institution images.
- Personalized Course Recommendations: AI tool: Give prospective students tailored course recommendations, based on their career goals and prior educational achievements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#003049) to convey trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Very light blue (#D4E7F0), providing a clean, modern feel.
- Accent color: Light blue (#4095c6), to highlight calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for clear and accessible reading.
- Use simple, professional icons relevant to course categories and features.
- A clean, well-structured layout with clear sections for courses, testimonials, and contact information.
